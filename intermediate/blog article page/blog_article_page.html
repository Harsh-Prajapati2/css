This is the first paragraph of my blog post. We are learning about semantic HTML and how it helps structure a webpage. Tags like <article>, <header>, and <footer> give meaning to our content, which is great for accessibility and search engines.

In this post, we will explore several key tags. It's important to understand that these tags don't just *look* different (in fact, many look the same by default), but they *tell* the browser what kind of content they hold.
